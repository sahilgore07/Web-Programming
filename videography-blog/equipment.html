Recommended Videography Equipment

  • Camera: Sony A7S III or Canon EOS R5 - Great low-light performance and 4K/8K video recording.
  • Lens: 24-70mm f/2.8 - Versatile zoom lens for different shooting scenarios.
  • Microphone: Rode VideoMic Pro+ - High-quality directional audio for professional sound.
  • Lighting: Neewer LED Video Lights - Adjustable brightness and color temperature.
  • Stabilizer: DJI Ronin-S Gimbal - Smooth camera movements for cinematic shots.
  • Drone: DJI Mavic 3 - Aerial shots with 4K video and excellent stabilization.
  • External Recorder: Atomos Ninja V - Captures high-bitrate footage for better post-production quality.
  • Editing Software: Adobe Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, or Final Cut Pro - Professional video editing tools.
  • Memory Cards: SanDisk Extreme Pro 256GB - High-speed storage for 4K video recording.
  • Audio Recorder: Zoom H6 - High-quality external audio recording for interviews and sound effects.
  • Camera Bag: Lowepro ProTactic 450 AW - Protects and organizes gear for travel and shoots.
